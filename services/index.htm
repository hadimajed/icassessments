Management system certification

Intercontinental Assessments is a globally recognized independent certification body which provides management system certifications to all industry domains such as construction, manufacturing, hospitality, IT, BT, banking, healthcare etc.

Trainings

At ICA we have expert trainers who comes from different industry domains with rich knowledge on ISO standards, we deliver quality training programs to corporates and interested individuals

Third party inspections and audit

We have well experienced auditors spread across in many countries, to ensure compliance in supply chain we provide third party audit and inspections services on behalf of organizations
