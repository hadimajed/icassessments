  • ISO 14001 is a standard published by International Organization for Standardization (ISO) which specifies requirements for Environmental Management System in organizations.
  • The standard under its 10 clauses specifies that an organization should identify the environmental aspects and its impacts (direct or indirect) on the environment.
  • In the modern era of rapid industrialization globally the society is expecting the organizations to be completely aware of the adverse impacts caused on environment from their operations.
  • ISO 14001 is considered as a basic standard which provides guidelines nothing directly prescriptive but a level of criterial guidelines for any organizations to comply for achieving best results
  • To gain confidence from society and individuals and to care for environment, the organizations are interested to comply with ISO 14001 standard.
