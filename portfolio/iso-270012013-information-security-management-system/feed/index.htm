ISO 27001:2013 - Information Security Management System

  • ISO 27001 is the standard which sets out the requirements for Information Security Management System
  • The standard under its 10 clauses neatly describes the guidelines to achieve security of information in the management system
  • In ISO 27001 the term information is not only referred to the data stored in electronic communication devices, here information is referred as an asset including the business ideas, drawings, knowledge imparted, paper media etc.
  • ISO 27001 Information Security Management System (ISMS) is a management system standard, it can be implemented in any type of organization, irrespective of their nature and size of the business
  • ISO 27001 can be implemented along with other standards like 9001, 14001, 45001 etc.
  • ISO 27001 standard has simple guidelines, annex SL structure and descriptive controls present in annex A of the standard which makes easy to understand the terms and implement the standard in their organizations. The core knowledge of their own business is the only crucial factor necessary to facilitate implementing this standard.
