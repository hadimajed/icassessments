  • ISO 45001 is a standard published by International organization for standardization (ISO) in the year 2018 which is dedicatedly specifies requirements for occupational health and safety management system
  • Earlier there was no particular standard from ISO for occupational health and safety and occupational health and safety assessment series OHSAS 18001 was widely adopted
  • ISO 45001 follows annex SL structure and can be implemented in any type of organization together with other management system standards like ISO 9001 QMS and 14001 EMS etc.
  • ISO 45001 has high level structure, standard languages and definitions, risk-based approach, strategic direction, leadership and commitment, monitoring of objectives, procurement and outsourced processes, business processes integration
  • The new standard is comprehensive and demanding on auditors, consultants, implementers, management and trainers as issues are raised with reviewed terms and definitions. New definitions given to: consultation, contractor, corrective action, injury and ill health, incident, hazard, risk, worker, participation, outsource, etc.
