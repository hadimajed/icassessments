ISO 20000-1:2018 - IT Service Management System
  • ISO 2000-1 is an International standard published by ISO for Service Management System (SMS)
  • Designed to help organizations ensure that they provide effective service management
  • Service management for internal and/or external customers
  • Closely follows ITIL framework
  • While individuals are ITIL certified, organizations are ISO 20000 certified
  • Produces higher IT service quality and increases customer satisfaction
  • Improves user productivity and assist your organization in benchmarking its IT service management
  • It could be used for all types of services (IT & non IT)
  • Provides higher customer confidence and creates a framework for independent assessment
  • Provides the ability to bid on certain Government & Commercial contracts
